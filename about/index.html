What is this?

Everyone has ideas. Some people show others, and others keep them private.

It's time for me to start putting myself and my ideas out there for others to see, appreciate, and think about.

For some people, this might be a bad idea. For me, it might be as well, but I learn best by doing, so let's do.

Here I organize my thoughts and show off my work, which if at least one person appreciates, will make this all worth it.
